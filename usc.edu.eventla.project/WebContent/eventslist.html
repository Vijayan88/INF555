Green Growth, Smart Growth

Staple Center, Los Angeles, 10.00 am

Green Growth, Smart Growth: A New Approach to Economics, Innovation, and the Environment Lecture by Ralf Fuecks. Ralf Fcks book Green Growth, Smart Growth: A New Approach to Economics, Innovation and the Environment proposes that we find ourselves at a crossroads between environmental disaster and a new industrial revolution: a shift from the ruthless exploitation of nature toward cooperation with it. Fcks asserts that decoupling economic growth from environmental consumption is an ambitious goal, but also an achievable one. In presenting Green Growth, Smart Growth via a multimedia presentation and discussion, Fcks outlines a way forward in this great transformation, and does so in the conviction that the dangers posed by climate change can be overcome through a new approach to economics, innovation and proactive policymaking. Ralf Fuecks has been a member of the Executive Board of the Heinrich Bll Foundation since 1996.
