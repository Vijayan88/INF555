About

No one knows the city like Events LA

  • What We Do

    Events LA is the trusted global platform that inspires and enables people to experience the best of the city. No one knows the city like Events LA because we’ve been discovering the brilliant and searching out the secret and extraordinary. If you want to know about food, attractions, art, culture, shopping and nightlife, then Events LA is your social companion!

  • Online

    Events LA online platforms give the audience the best in recommendations and reviews as well as a comprehensive arts, entertainments and events listings. We inspire people and help them find what they’re looking for, enable them to do it via booking platforms, and allow them to share their experiences with other members of the Events LA community!

  • Mobile App

    Delivering inspiration on the go, our apps for iOS, Android and Windows devices help users find the best things to do. Geo-location enabled, the apps also allow easy-booking of tickets and restaurant tables, as well as searches by area, Editor’s picks and Most Popular!

  • The Founding of Events LA

    Fascinated with the culture of Los Angeles and a need for reliable information about what was going on, Ayushi Agarwal, Lailu Philip and Vijayan B founded events LA in February 2016 during thier course project for USC. They are passionate about Event LA and have a vision that goes far beyond simply reproducing features from the magazine, delivering a vast array of unique local content and cultural insight, and enabling consumer bookings and a wide opportunity to resonate with its audience!
